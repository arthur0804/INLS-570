The UNC School of Information and Library Science (SILS) has been contracted to operate the EPA Library in Research Triangle Park (EPA-RTP Library) for over 40 years. The EPA-RTP Library is one of 25 libraries in the EPA National Library Network, and the only one staffed by university employees. In addition to the full-time staff members, the SILS and the EPA Library partnership provides six to eight internships for SILS master’s students each year.
